The '/dev/sdb1' means  "into the first partition".
With '/dev/sdb' it is "into the whole device, starting at the begin of the device"
Retry with
   sudo dd bs=4M if=~/Downloads/debian-mac-testing-amd64-netinst.iso of=/dev/sdb && sync
